Uber Trips Data Analysis Dashboard

A mobility analytics project using 50,000 Uber trip records to understand travel patterns, service efficiency, pricing behavior, cancellations, city performance, and customer usage trends through Tableau dashboards.

What The Project Talks About

This project analyzes Uber trip data to identify peak demand hours, trip completion and cancellation patterns, fare and distance trends, and operational signals that can help improve driver allocation and service reliability.

The dashboard has a business performance overview page for high-level operations and a deep-dive page for cancellation behavior, trip duration, hourly fare changes, and distance distribution.

My Contribution

I prepared the dashboard narrative, selected business and operational KPIs, organized the analysis into overview and deep-dive pages, and translated trip-level data into insights about demand, supply pressure, fare behavior, and customer travel patterns.

Project Workflow

1. Dataset Review

Studied trip ID, driver ID, rider ID, city, timestamps, coordinates, distance, fare, status, and payment method.

2. KPI Design

Defined total trips, revenue, average fare, average distance, trip status, hourly demand, and city performance.

3. Deep Dive Analysis

Analyzed cancelled trips by hour, average trip duration by hour, hourly fare, and distance distribution.

4. Dashboarding

Built two Tableau dashboard pages for business overview and operational drill-down.

Key Insights

  • Peak demand occurs during morning and evening hours.
  • Higher cancellations during peak hours indicate supply-demand imbalance.
  • Trip duration rises during busy periods, likely reflecting traffic congestion.
  • Fare increases during high-demand periods, showing dynamic pricing behavior.
  • Most trips are short-distance, suggesting strong intra-city travel demand.

Recommendations

  • Increase driver availability during morning and evening peaks.
  • Track cancellation-heavy hours as a supply planning metric.
  • Use city-level demand performance to guide targeted driver incentives.
  • Monitor short-trip pricing and pickup efficiency for better customer experience.
