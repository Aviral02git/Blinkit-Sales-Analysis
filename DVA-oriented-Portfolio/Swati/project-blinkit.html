What The Project Talks About

This project studies Blinkit-style quick commerce operations to answer practical business questions: how revenue is performing, where delivery delays happen, which customers drive revenue, what causes poor satisfaction, and how operations can be optimized.

The dashboard is organized into business overview, delivery efficiency, and customer insights so leadership can connect revenue strength with service quality and retention risks.

My Contribution

I contributed to the full analytics workflow, moving from raw operational data through ETL, feature engineering, clean dataset preparation, exploratory analysis, statistical analysis, and final Tableau dashboard storytelling.

Project Workflow

1. Raw Data Review

Mapped revenue, orders, delivery, customer, partner, store, payment, and feedback fields.

2. ETL & Feature Engineering

Prepared clean analysis fields for revenue, margin, delays, distance, delivery time, and customer segments.

3. EDA & Statistical Analysis

Studied seasonal revenue, delay behavior, customer satisfaction, repeat rate, and feedback distribution.

4. Dashboarding

Built three Tableau sections for executive overview, delivery operations, and customer experience insights.

Key Insights

  • Revenue is strong, but delivery and customer experience issues create business risk.
  • About 62% of orders are delayed, making delivery efficiency the biggest operational concern.
  • Peak hours cause the highest delays, suggesting capacity and staffing pressure.
  • A small set of stores and delivery partners contribute heavily to delay problems.
  • Approximately 63% of customer feedback is negative while the repeat customer rate remains high.

Recommendations

  • Optimize peak-hour delivery capacity and staffing.
  • Monitor underperforming stores and delivery partners separately.
  • Improve customer experience to reduce negative feedback and rating pressure.
  • Focus retention and growth strategy on regular and premium customer segments.
